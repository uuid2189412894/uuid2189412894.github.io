Yang Guo the Crayfish

Yang Guo isn’t just any crayfish—this little legend has the grit and determination we can all relate to. When faced with the boiling fate of a hot pot, Yang Guo made the ultimate sacrifice, tearing off its own claw to break free. It’s a reminder of what we sometimes go through ourselves—having to sacrifice, grit our teeth, and, yes, maybe even rip off an arm or two just to push forward. Yang Guo embodies the relentless spirit that’s behind every hard-fought journey, including sending this coin. Here’s to resilience, courage, and a crayfish that truly gets it.
